About the app

This app can recognize handwritten hebrew letters without sofits. Draw a letter on the canvas above and press the "Predict" button to see a prediction. Press the "Clear" button to clear the canvas and draw a letter again. "Show more information" button reveals detailed predictions by all models.

Collecting the data

The drawn image is saved in a temporary folder which is emptied at the end of each session. By default, the drawn image is used to additionally train models and futher improve the prediction quality. If you don't want this to happen, uncheck the flag below and no information will be collected.
